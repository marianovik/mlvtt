This privacy policy applies to the Teleprompter app for mobile devices created by Mariia Novik as a free service. This service is provided "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data remains locally on your device.

Data Storage

All scripts and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. The developer does not have access to your scripts, videos, or any other data created within the Application.

Permissions Used

The Application requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: Required to record videos while reading your teleprompter scripts
  • Microphone: Required to record audio when creating videos
  • Photo Library: Required to save your recorded videos to your device's photo library

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the Application. No data is transmitted, shared, or uploaded.

Third Party Access

The Application does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking. Your data is never shared with third parties.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. The developer does not collect or retain any user data. To delete all app data, simply uninstall the Application from your device.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application does not collect any personally identifiable information.

Security

Since all data remains on your device and the Application does not transmit any information, your data security is managed by your device's built-in iOS security features.
